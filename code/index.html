How does it work?

This site works by creating a circle with 5km radius from the point your specify. This is 5km "as the bird flies" as the maximum limit, not 5km by any road or means of transport. The 5km area of travel by road may be much smaller.

 

Follow the advice of your local health authorities above all else. For Victoria, that is the Department of Health and Human Services. Stay safe
